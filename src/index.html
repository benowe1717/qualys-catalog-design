Levels of Training

Foundation

These classes are our most basic. They introduce you to either basic security concepts or high-level Qualys technology. These courses do not have a competency exam. Completing a Foundation course gives you a completion certificate. These certificates do not expire.

Onboarding

Onboarding Training is designed to get new customers moving as quickly as possible. It is like a crash course in getting up to speed quickly. These courses do not have a competency exam. Completing an Onboarding course gives you a completion certificate. These certificates do not expire.

Specialist

After Onboarding, you should attend Certified Specialist Training. We have recommended appropriately ordered learning paths. Certified Specialist Training can be self-paced or, in some cases, instructor-led. After completing the training, there is an exam associated with the course. Certified Specialist certificates expire and require recertification. A completion certificate is given for completing the training, and a specialist certificate is given for passing the exam.
